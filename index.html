Slice Of My life

Hello, every one my name is Abenezer Fekadu and i was born in a small town called Gursum, from my mother Azalech Ababa and my father Fekadu Tefera. so when I was born I wasn't alone, i had a twin brother whose name Natnael Fekadu. when I was on the age of 5 me and my brother started school, but then we didnt know what it was. After learning in KG for two years my family moved on to another town called babile, so we have to move too. Here we continued our school and made it to 1st class. when I lived in babile I used to have a lot of friends, we used to play a lot, even watched movies together.
